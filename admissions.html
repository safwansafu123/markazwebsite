Academic Programs

Choose Your Path of Learning

Foundation · 2 Years
Hifzul Qur'an

A dedicated program for Quranic memorization with proper Tajweed, Tarteel, and recitation skills under expert Qurra.

⏳ 2 Years 🎯 Age 10+ 📜 Sanad Provided
Secondary · 3 Years
Islamic Foundation Course

Comprehensive grounding in Arabic language, basic Islamic sciences, and the classical texts of the Dars-e-Nizami syllabus.

⏳ 3 Years 🎯 Age 13+ 📜 Certificate
Higher · 6 Years
Aalimiyya / Fazilat

Our flagship Aalim program covering all major Islamic disciplines — Fiqh, Tafsir, Hadith, Aqeedah, and Arabic — following the traditional Dars-e-Nizami.

⏳ 6 Years 🎯 Age 16+ 📜 Degree Equivalent
Specialization · 2 Years
Takhassus fil Fiqh

An advanced post-graduation specialization in Islamic jurisprudence, Fatwa writing, and contemporary Fiqh issues for Aalim graduates.

⏳ 2 Years 🎯 Aalim Graduates 📜 Mufti Certification

Submit Application

02
