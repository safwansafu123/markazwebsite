Who We Are

A Beacon of Islamic Learning in Kerala

Markazul Uloomil Islami, Nilambur is a distinguished Islamic educational institution in the historic town of Nilambur, Malappuram district, Kerala — one of the most respected centres of traditional Islamic learning in the region.

The institution operates under the name KT Manu Musliyar Sharee'th and Arts College, established in 2009, registered under Reg. No. 279/13, affiliated with Jamia Nooriya Arabiya.

It honours the legacy of Late Jb. K.T. Manu Musliyar — a towering Islamic scholar and General Secretary of Samastha Kerala Islam Matha Vidyabhyasa Board — whose lifelong dedication to Islamic education remains the guiding spirit of Markaz.

Our Journey

History & Milestones

Foundation
KT Manu Musliyar's Legacy
Late Jb. K.T. Mohammed Musliyar dedicated his life to Islamic scholarship. As General Secretary of Samastha Kerala Islam Matha Vidyabhyasa Board, he laid the foundation for Islamic education across Kerala.
2009
Establishment of Markaz
Markazul Uloomil Islami was established in Nilambur, named in honour of KT Manu Musliyar, with a mission to provide high-quality Islamic education in the Samastha Kerala Jamiyyathul Ulama tradition.
2013
Official Registration
The institution received official registration under Reg. No. 279/13, and affiliation with Jamia Nooriya Arabiya strengthened its academic standing across Kerala's Islamic educational network.
Growth
Expanding Facilities
Markaz expanded its infrastructure — library, computer lab, smart classrooms, residential hostels, and a grand mosque — all funded through generous alumni and community contributions.
Today
A Thriving Community
Markaz today is a vibrant hub of Islamic learning and spiritual development — nurturing hundreds of students who serve as scholars, educators, and leaders across Kerala and beyond.
