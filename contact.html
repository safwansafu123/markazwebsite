FAQ

Common Questions

When are admissions open for the next academic year?
+
Admissions for the 2025–26 academic year are now open. Applications can be submitted online or in person at the admissions office. The last date for applications is typically April 30th.
What documents are required for the admission process?
+
You will need your previous academic certificates, transfer certificate, birth certificate, two passport-size photographs, and a valid government-issued ID. International students may need additional documentation.
Are scholarships or fee waivers available?
+
Yes. We offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. Students with exceptional academic records or from financially disadvantaged backgrounds are encouraged to apply. Contact the admissions office for details.
Is hostel accommodation available for outstation students?
+
We provide comfortable and secure residential facilities for students who are not local. The hostel includes study rooms, communal spaces, and meals. Limited seats are available, so early registration is recommended.
How can alumni stay connected with the institution?
+
Alumni can register on our Alumni Network portal, attend annual reunions, and join department-specific WhatsApp and Telegram groups. We regularly host alumni lectures and career networking events.
