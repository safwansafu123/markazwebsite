About Muaawina

A Brotherhood Built to Last

بسم الله

Muaawina — meaning "helpers and supporters" — is the official alumni association of Markazul Uloomi Islami, Nilambur. Born from the bonds forged in classrooms, prayer halls, and hostel corridors, Muaawina keeps those bonds alive and purposeful.

Our graduates carry the Markaz spirit into every corner of the world — serving as Imams, scholars, educators, doctors, engineers, and community leaders.

🕌

Spiritual Brotherhood

Strengthening bonds forged through shared worship, learning, and campus life.

🌍

Global Network

Connecting Markaz graduates across Kerala, India, and 18+ countries worldwide.

🤝

Community Service

Organising welfare, scholarships, and da'wah initiatives together.

Our Journey

Milestones of Muaawina

تاريخنا

From humble beginnings to a vibrant global network — the story of Muaawina mirrors the growth of Markaz itself.


Know More →
1993
Markaz Established
Markazul Uloomi Islami opens its doors in Nilambur with a vision to blend traditional Islamic scholarship with modern education.
2000
First Alumni Cohort Reunites
The earliest graduates gather for the first formal reunion, planting the seeds for what would become the Muaawina Alumni Network.
2010
Muaawina Formally Founded
The alumni association is officially established with elected office-bearers, a constitution, and a commitment to alumni welfare.
2018
International Chapters Launched
With graduates across the Gulf, Malaysia, and Europe, Muaawina expands globally with active international chapters.
2024
Digital Era: @muaawina_updates
The official Instagram account launches, connecting hundreds of alumni through updates, highlights, and shared memories.
